Tikita Online Shop

Thanks for visiting our shop!

Tikita is an online shop born in PobleNou (Barcelona) from the passion for art design and the love for local symbols.

We believe that symbols of our cities and districts are the backbone of our culture, and should be passed along to next generations to carry a vivid memory of our history and traditions.

We started from Gigants and the Bestie, as they have been core symbols of our city for centuries. Through our art we hope to connect people, including the youngest generations, to the Catalan heritage and create a vivid memory of our history.
